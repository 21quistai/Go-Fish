Go Fish

How to play go fish

During a turn the player asks another player if they have a particular rank of card. For example, the player may ask player 1 if she has any nines. If player 1 has a nine, then they have to give a nine to that player. If player 1 doesn't have any nines, then they says "go fish". When they say "go fish" you draw a card from the pile

First start by selecting a card you want to ask someone for. After that you must select a player to ask by clicking on the button next to them that says 'ASK ME'
